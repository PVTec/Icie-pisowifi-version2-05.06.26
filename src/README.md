# Icie Wifi Portal: Overview & Features

The Icie Wifi Portal is a comprehensive web application designed to create an engaging and rewarding experience for your wifi users. It transforms a standard internet service into an interactive platform with a virtual economy, games, and deep customization.

## Core Features:

### User Authentication & Profile
- **Secure Sign-up & Login**: A robust authentication system allows users to create and access their accounts.
- **One Account Per Device**: To ensure fair distribution of rewards, new account creation is limited to one per device.
- **Customizable Profiles**: Users can personalize their experience by choosing from a selection of avatars and setting a custom display name.

### Credit-Based Economy
A complete virtual currency system where users can earn, spend, and manage "Credits."
- **Earning Credits**:
    - **Welcome Bonus**: A one-time gift of **100 credits** for every new user.
    - **Daily Login Bonus**: A recurring reward of **5 credits** that can be claimed every 36 hours.
    - **Redeemable Codes**: Users can redeem special "Credit Codes" to instantly boost their balance.
    - **Monthly Events**: Complete challenges to earn significant credit bonuses.
    - **Play & Earn**: Win credits by playing games in the Icie Arena.
- **Spending Credits**: Credits are the primary currency for purchasing WiFi vouchers and exclusive cosmetic items.

### Dynamic Shop System
- **Voucher Shop**: A marketplace where users spend credits to buy various WiFi vouchers. It includes a special **97% discount** on the user's first-ever purchase to encourage engagement.
- **Theme & Effect Shop**: A cosmetic shop where users can purchase and equip items:
    - **Visual Themes**: A variety of themes including Light, Dark, "Bubbles Light," and exclusive BGM-synced themes (Golden, Takedown, How It's Done).
    - **Animated Effects**: Standalone background effects, like the popular **Bubble Effect**, that can be equipped over any theme.

### Trial System for Premium Cosmetics
To give users a taste of premium features, the portal includes a trial system:
- **Bubble Effect Trial**: New users can activate a **3-hour free trial** for the animated Bubble Effect.
- **BGM Theme Trial**: A special **36-hour trial** for exclusive, limited-edition themes that are synchronized with background music, accessible via the user's profile page.

### Icie Arena - Play & Earn Game Center
- **Exclusive Access**: A gaming section accessible only to users connected to the "Icie Wifi" network or those with a special access pass.
- **Five Unique Games**: Users can spend credits to play and win bigger rewards in five different games:
    1.  **Lucky Draw**: A classic slot machine game.
    2.  **Witch Hunt**: A game of chance to find reward cards.
    3.  **Terminal Code**: A logic-based code-guessing game.
    4.  **Power Balance**: A test of timing and precision.
    5.  **Last Stand**: A reflex-based target-hitting game.

### Credit Investment
- **Strategic Growth**: A dedicated section where users can invest their credits to potentially grow their balance over time.
- **Dynamic Risk/Reward**: The longer an investment is active, the higher the potential for growth (and loss), rewarding strategic, long-term plays.
- **Wallet & Bank**: Users manage funds between their main balance, a safe "Wallet," and an active "Bank" for investments.

### Universal Network Access
- **Play From Anywhere**: A unique feature allowing users to bypass the local WiFi requirement for the Arena.
- **Access Codes**: By entering a special administrative code on the dedicated "Network Access" page, users can gain permanent universal access, enabling them to play from any network, including other WiFi networks or mobile data.

### Advanced Personalization
- **Background Music Player**: An in-app BGM player with multiple tracks, including limited-edition music that syncs with exclusive themes.
- **Mix & Match**: Users can combine any purchased theme with any purchased animated effect for a truly personalized portal experience.
